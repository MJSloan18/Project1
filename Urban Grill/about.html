About Urban Grill

RFMC - Urban Grill is located on 320 E. 9th Street in downtown Charlotte, NC. We set out to choose a desireable location that would offer our guests and investors a magnificent view of the surroundings and skyline of the city. Here at the Urban Grill, we are dedicated to providing a memorable experience to each and every individual that may stumble accross our website, or walk through our doors.

All of our guests will soon realize the plethora of entertainment possibilites in the Charlotte area. We have partnered with TicketMaster and now offer our guests the ability to book tickets from our website for various events around the Charlotte area. Endulge in anything from tailgating along with Carolina Panthers fans, enjoy a Charlotte Knights baseball inning, catch the first half of the Hornets at the Time Warner Cable Arena, or even experience your favorite musicians live in concert. Check out the Bonus page for upcoming events.

We understand the importance of options and want to support anyone with a calorie conscious diet; therefore we have partnerd with Nutritionix in order for our chefs to have the ability to incorporate a total calorie count per menu item for our guests.

We offer the best service around, come join us! Great food. Great people. Providing you with the experience of a lifetime, our mission is to fulfill your every need and send you on your way with a new sublime state of mind!
